# Sales Dashboard – Power BI

## About This Project
An end-to-end Power BI dashboard built on retail sales data from 
January 2024 to August 2024, covering 309 transactions across 
9 Indian cities and 7 states.

## Dataset Highlights
- 309 orders | 20 columns
- Cities: Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Jaipur
- Categories: Electronics, Peripherals, Accessories
- Products: Laptop, Monitor, Tablet, Phone, Webcam, Keyboard, Accessories
- Customer Segments: Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze
- Salespersons: 8 team members tracked
- Payment Methods: UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, Cash
- Total Revenue: ₹3.36 Crore
- Avg Customer Rating: 3.71 / 5
- Repeat Customer Rate: 59.2%

## What This Dashboard Covers
- Revenue and quantity trends over time (Jan–Aug 2024)
- Sales breakdown by Category and Product
- Performance by Salesperson
- Customer Segment analysis (Platinum to Bronze)
- City and State-wise regional distribution
- Payment method preferences
- Customer Rating and Repeat Customer insights
- Discount impact on Net Sales
